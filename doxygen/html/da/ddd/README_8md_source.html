1 # MemFG
2 **MemFG** is a module written in Python, which allows us to
3 
4 - define user-custom models of dwarf spheroidal galaxies (dSphs),
5 - evaluate, test, and compare dSph models,
6 - search the parameter region of a given dSph,
7 - estimate J-factor PDFs or confidencial intervals.
8 
9 **MemFG** module is implemented to satisfy that
10 
11 - dSph models can be defined by using a specific model format in MemFG,
12 - all of the output files yielded by MemFG have a user-friendly and easily-reuseable format,
13 - statistical frameworks can be swicthed between such as frequentist statistics or Bayesian statistics.
14 - sampling algorithm can be switched among several MCMC or nested samplers.
